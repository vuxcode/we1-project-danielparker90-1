About London

History

Londons development spans thousands of years. Here are a few key moments:

  • Founded by the Romans around 50 AD as Londinium.
  • Survived the Great Fire of 1666 and was rebuilt under Christopher Wrens designs.
  • Flourished during the Industrial Revolution, becoming the worlds largest city in the 19th century.

Culture

Londons cultral life is incredbily vibrant. You will find:

  • The British Museum and the National Gallery offering free entry.
  • World-famous theatres like the Globe and West End venues.
  • Diverse cuisines from around the globe in every neighborhood.

Landmarks

Here are some must-see landmarks when visting London

  • The Tower of London: A historic fortress and former royal palace.
  • Buckingham Palace: The official residence of the monarch.
  • Big Ben and the Houses of Parliament: Iconic symbols of the UK government.
