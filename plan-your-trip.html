Plan Your Trip to London

Best Time to Visit

  • Spring (March to May) offers blooming parks and mild weather.
  • Autumn (September to November) has fewer crowds and pleasant temperatures.

Transportation

  • Use the Underground (Tube) for fast travel across the city.
  • London buses are convenient and scenic.
  • Rent bikes using Santander Cycles for short trips.

Accommodation

  • Luxury hotels are available in the West End and Kensington.
  • Budget options like hostels and guesthouses are in areas like Camden or Shoreditch.
  • Book early during peak seasons.

Local Tips

  • Get an Oyster card or contactless payment for cheaper travel.
  • Many top museums are free to enter.
  • Avoid peak hours (7-9 AM and 5-7 PM) when using public transport.
